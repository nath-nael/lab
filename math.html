    Learnng Objectives:

    2. Learn how to solve linear equations

    Summary :
    Algebra is a branch of mathematics that deals with symbols and the rules for manipulating those symbols. These symbols, often represented by letters, stand for numbers and other quantities in equations and formulas. Algebra provides a way to represent and solve problems involving unknown values.
